The New "Lipstick Effect"

A new kind of "Lipstick Effect" occurred in Asia during the past five years, as more and more Korean drama began to lead the trend in lipstick colors. Customers are more willing to buy the lipstick of same brands and colors with those used by actresses in the popular Korean dramas.
